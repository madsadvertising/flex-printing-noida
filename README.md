🚀 Types of Flex Printing
-------------------------

### 1\. Frontlit Flex

Frontlit flex is illuminated using external lighting placed in front of the printed surface.

#### Best For

*   Shop Sign Boards
    
*   Event Banners
    
*   Outdoor Advertising
    
*   Promotional Campaigns
    
*   Hoardings
    

#### Features

*   Affordable pricing
    
*   Waterproof material
    
*   Suitable for outdoor use
    
*   Fast production time
    

#### GSM Range

280–340 GSM

#### Average Price

₹8–20 per sq ft

### 2\. Backlit Flex

Backlit flex is designed for lightboxes and glow sign boards where lighting comes from behind the print.

#### Best For

*   Glow Sign Boards
    
*   Shopping Malls
    
*   Petrol Pumps
    
*   Airports
    
*   Premium Retail Stores
    

#### Features

*   Excellent night visibility
    
*   Vibrant colors
    
*   Premium appearance
    
*   Long lifespan
    

#### GSM Range

440–510 GSM

#### Average Price

₹25–60 per sq ft

### 3\. Star Flex

Star Flex is a premium-grade flex material known for better print quality and durability.

#### Best For

*   Corporate Branding
    
*   Premium Signage
    
*   Retail Stores
    
*   Long-Term Outdoor Advertising
    

#### Features

*   Premium finish
    
*   Better color reproduction
    
*   Higher durability
    
*   Strong material quality
    

#### GSM Range

380–550 GSM

#### Average Price

₹20–50 per sq ft

🎯 Choosing the Right Material
------------------------------

### Choose Frontlit If:

*   Budget is your priority
    
*   Temporary advertising campaigns
    
*   Outdoor banners and hoardings
    

### Choose Backlit If:

*   You need illuminated signage
    
*   Business operates at night
    
*   Premium branding is required
    

### Choose Star Flex If:

*   Long-term branding is important
    
*   Premium quality is needed
    
*   Better durability is required
